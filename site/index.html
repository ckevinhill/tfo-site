THE OFFICE

Meet the Tooth Fairies

Welcome to the Office of the Tooth Fairy! More accurately it should be called the Office of the Tooth Fairies as there are currently over 3000 different Tooth Fairy Agents currently employed by the office. The mission statement of the Tooth Fairy Office (or TFO as we like to call it) is to help children all over the world transition to adulthood by making the growing-up process more fun. As children grow they inevitably lose their "baby teeth" - we Agents of the Tooth Fairy Office collect these teeth and leave a small gift in exchange. We like to think this helps children look forward to losing teeth - and ultimately helps with the scary process of growing up.

In case you were wondering, Tooth Fairy Agents come in all shapes, sizes and colors. We are a very diverse, high-performance workforce. There are currently 3 different levels of Agent within the TFO:

Associate Tooth Fairy Agent - including new members to our team that are just starting out in their exciting Tooth Fairy career journey.

Tooth Fairy Agent - designation for a Tooth Fairy Agent once they have passed their probationary period.

Tooth Fairy Supervisory Agent - highly experienced Tooth Fairy Agents who oversee other Tooth Fairy Agents and Junior Agents. Typically a Tooth Fairy Supervisory Agent oversees a geographic region, making sure that deliveries are successful and responsible for mentoring more junior Agents.

And finally there is the Head Tooth Fairy who is responsible for running the Tooth Fairy Office as well as being responsible for cross-department information exchange with other organizations like the Santa Claus Office and the Easter Bunny Office. This Fairy is elected every 5 years based on voting from all currently active Tooth Fairy Supervisory Agents.

As an additional note, as we are bringing new Tooth Fairies onboard, sometimes they do make mistakes. Sometimes they forget to make a tooth pick-up, or leave a gift. Or sometimes they accidentally turn the family pet into a shoe or something weird like that. Young fairies are a bit unpredictable. If you experience any issues with your Tooth Fairy delivery please make sure to let us know via the complaints form above! Your feedback is highly appreciated.

Finally, two more things we would like to share with you about the current Tooth Fairy Office:

First, No - we don't keep all the teeth we collect, that would be super creepy. We make sure to dispose of them in a environmentally friendly way and do recycle alot of the enamel into a wide-range of fairy products including chairs, tables, structural support beams and many house-hold items like spoons & bowls.

Second, any attempt to capture a Tooth Fairy Agent or impede an Agents work in anyway is considered a "naughty act" and will immediately be reported to all other Offices including The Office of Santa Claus.
